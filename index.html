How to Play

Choose two cards and if the emojis underneath match, you will receive a point.
There are a total of 8 avaliable on easy level, 10 points on medium level and 12 points on hard level.
Completing the board is the quickest time will earn extra points and if you are fast enough, a place on illustrous leaderboard.
The timer will begin once you flip your first card.
Good Luck!
