Gang of Eight Presents

how to play!

Choose two cards and if the emojis underneath match, you will receive a point.
There are a total of 8 available on easy level, 10 points on medium level and 12 points on hard level.
Completing the board is the quickest time will earn extra points and if you are fast enough, a place on illustrious leader board.
The timer will begin once you flip your first card.
Good Luck!
